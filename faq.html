Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?
Browse our featured collection or shop all products, then click "Add to Cart" on your desired items. Your cart will save your selections while you shop.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash, bank transfer, and select digital payment options. Please contact us for payment details and instructions.
How long does delivery take?
Orders are processed within 1–2 days and delivered within 3–5 business days in Jamaica. For custom orders, delivery times may vary.
Can I request a custom order?
Absolutely! Use our Contact page or email us with your custom request, and we’ll be happy to assist.
How do I leave a review?
You can leave a 5-star review on each product in our featured collection by clicking the stars under the product after your purchase.
